The Design Process Page: Wireframes

This section highlights the visual design elements used throughout the project, focusing on layout, color schemes, and visual hierarchy. It showcases my design process, which began with the creation of detailed wireframes that outlined the structure and functionality of each page, ensuring a seamless user experience. Additionally, I present my design style guide, defining the typography, color palette, and graphic elements utilized throughout the project. This guide not only maintains consistency but also reflects the brand's identity and values. By incorporating feedback and iterative testing, I refined my designs to create an engaging and cohesive visual narrative, where every aspect, from initial sketches to final visuals, plays a crucial role in achieving an effective user interface.
